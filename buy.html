Token Purchase / Participation Units

The GoldStory project offers interested parties the opportunity to acquire tokens called GoldStory (GS), which represent symbolic participation units in the gold trading business. These tokens are an innovative way to get involved in a tangible project backed by physical assets, offering investors a path to benefit from the commercial operation's generated profits.

What does purchasing a GS token represent?

Each GS token represents a proportional participation unit in the net profitability generated by the project's gold trading operations. By acquiring these tokens, investors are not purchasing legal shares or equity in the company; instead, they are making a voluntary donation that entitles them to participate in the economic profit distribution as the business operation progresses.

Price and Availability

  • The fixed token price is $0.60 USD.
  • A total of 2,000,000 GS tokens will be issued.
  • The minimum purchase amount will be defined according to investment and participation cycles.

Purchase Process

The acquisition of tokens is made through a monetary donation via the project's official platform. This donation becomes the basis for the proportional allocation of tokens, which will be credited directly to the investor's digital wallet.

Holder Rights and Benefits

  • Right to receive a proportional share of the business’s net profits.
  • Periodic distribution of benefits based on financial results.
  • Participation based on the number of tokens relative to the total distributed.
  • Estimated return per project of X%, according to projections.
  • Tokens do not confer legal or ownership rights in the company.

Terms and Considerations

Investing through the purchase of GS tokens is considered a voluntary donation for the purpose of profit participation and does not constitute a regulated financial instrument. Participants are encouraged to read the project's documentation carefully and understand the inherent risks. The GoldStory team is committed to transparency, offering periodic and accessible reports to all token holders.
